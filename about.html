Our Mission

We believe the gig economy has been exploited. Major platforms like AirTasker, Fiverr, and Upwork take 20-30% commissions, while taskers and creators struggle to earn a living. This isn't fair.

FairTasker exists to prove that a better platform is possible. One where taskers keep what they earn. Where job posters get quality work without predatory fees. Where transparency and fairness aren't buzzwords—they're the foundation.

Our Values

🤝 Fairness First

We don't profit from taskers' desperation. Our business model works when yours does, with transparent, ethical pricing.

💰 Transparent Earnings

No hidden fees. No surprise commissions. You know exactly what you earn before you claim a job. Every dollar is accounted for.

🔐 Security & Trust

Firebase-backed infrastructure, secure payments, and protected transactions. Your data is yours alone.

🌍 Local First

Starting in Australia (will expand globally), we're focused on meaningful local work. No race-to-the-bottom global pricing.

⚡ Simplicity

Post. Claim. Work. Get paid. No convoluted dashboards or confusing contract terms.

FAQ

Is FairTasker really free for taskers?

Mostly yes. There's a $5 claim fee paid by the job poster. Taskers keep 100% of agreed earnings. No hidden percentages.

How does FairTasker make money?

We take 30% of the claim fee initially, reinvesting into platform development. Future: subscription plans for premium features (optional).

When does payment happen?

Currently in beta. Full payment processing (Stripe) coming post-launch. Taskers get paid within 2 business days.

Is it safe?

Yes. Firebase security. encrypted data. escrow model in development. Both parties protected.

Only Australia?

Currently yes. We're focused on Australian gig workers. Expansion planned once we nail the local market.
